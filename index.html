About me

Hello, I'm Thinh, you can call me Finn.

Currently, I'm on my journey to become an Economist, specializing in Monetary Economics and International Macroeconomics.

I aim to deepen my understanding of foreign exchange interventions and monetary policy, particularly through the lens of the Mundell-Fleming impossible trinity, which highlights the trade-offs between exchange rate stability, capital mobility, and monetary independence. In particular, I look forward to exploring the dynamics of the exchange rate regime for a small open economy with a huge foreign capital influx. My first step would be to find myself accepted into a decent master's program in economics.

I earned my Distinction Bachelor's in Economics and Finance from RMIT University Vietnam in 2023. In 2024, I also finished a Pre-Master's program from DEPOCEN.
